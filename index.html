Profile

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Management Science and Engineering from Tongji SEM.

My research interests include innovation management and knowledge management as follows:

  • Innovation Management: Open innovation, Responsible innovation, Innovation ecosystem
  • Knowledge and Information Fields: Knowledge co-creation, User-generated content, Online communities, Open source contributions
  • Digital and AI Fields: Digital Platforms, Human-robot interaction, Generative AI
  • Methodologies: fsQCA, Topic modeling, Systems dynamics, Meta-analysis, Evolutionary game model

I have published papers in leading journals such as Management Review, Operations Research and Management Science, Systems Engineering, and Utilities Policy. I am proficient in various research tools such as Matlab, SPSS, Python, QCA, Vensim, Nvivo, etc.
